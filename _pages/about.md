I am a recently returned Peace Corps Timor-Leste volunteer and PhD student in linguistics at the University of Hawai'i at Mānoa. <br />

My research interests focus on the documentation of the languages of Timor-Leste, with a primary focus on the Midiki language, which is spoken in the Venilale Administrative Post. I am particularly interested in the historical contact between Midiki, an Austronesian language, and the non-Austronesian (or Papuan) languages spoken in Venilale and the surrounding areas. <br />
