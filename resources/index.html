non-Austronesian (Timor-Alor-Pantar)

Makasae TAP

First steps towards a grammar of Makasae: a language of East Timor Huber, Juliette (2008)

MA Dissertation Sketch grammar of Makasae based on the variety spoken in the subdistrict of Ossu, Viqueque district (southern Makasae).

Describing Makasae : a trans-New Guinea language of East Timor Correia, Aderito (2011)

Dissertation Grammar of Makasae writen by Timorese linguist Aderito Correia as his dissertation at Western Sydney University, based on the author's native variety of Makasae spoken in the subdistrict of Laga, Baucau district. Includes a Makasae-English, English-Makasae word list.

Makalero and Makasae Huber, Juliette (2017)

Book chapter Description and comparison of Makalero and Makasae. The fifth chapter of the second volume of The Papuan Languages of Timor, Alor and Pantar, edited by Antoinette Schapper (pg. 267-351).

A Spatial Odyssey: Referring to Space in Makasai Brotherson, Anna (2003)

Honours thesis Honours thesis from Australian National University describing the spatial reference system of Makasae. Difficult to find digitally.

Aspectos gramaticais da língua makasae de timor-leste : fonologia, morfologia e sintaxe Fogaça, Jessé Silveira (2016)

Dissertation Dissertation from Universidade de Brasília featuring a description of Makasae phonology, morphology, and syntax in Portuguese.

Makalero TAP

A grammar of Makalero: a Papuan language of East Timor Huber, Juliette (2011)

Dissertation Descriptive grammar of Makalero as spoken in the subdistrict of Iliomar, Lautém district. Written by Juliette Huber as her dissertation at Universiteit Leiden in the Netherlands. Includes a lengthy Makalero-English, English-Makalero word list.

Makalero and Makasae Huber, Juliette (2017)

Book chapter Description and comparison of Makalero and Makasae. The fifth chapter of the second volume of The Papuan Languages of Timor, Alor and Pantar, edited by Antoinette Schapper (pg. 267-351).

Vocabulariu: Makalero-Tetun-Portugues-Ingles Pinto, Thomas Mariano (2012)

Dictionary Four language Makalero dictionary produced by the Timor Lorosa'e-Nippon Culture Center. Difficult to find digitally.
